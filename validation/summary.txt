this folder is help to wright busniess logic to validate fields witch is comming from client side 

user.validation.js ------->
1 registerValidation — validates name, email, password for registration.
2 loginValidation — validates email, password, role for login.
3 updateProfileValidation — validates name/email updates.
4 changePasswordValidation — validates old/new password fields.
5 resetPasswordValidation — validates email for reset flow.
6 setNewPasswordValidation — validates new password for forgotten-password set.
7 verifyEmailValidation — validates email for verification.
8 forgotPasswordValidation — validates email for forgot-password.


items.validation.js -------->
1 addItemValidation — validates fields when adding lost/found items (title, description, status, category).
2 updateItemValidation — validates updates to an item.
3 deleteItemValidation — validates itemId for deletion.
4 getItemValidation — validates itemId for retrieval.
5 listItemsValidation — validates filters/pagination (status, category, page, limit).
